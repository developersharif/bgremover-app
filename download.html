System Requirements

Make sure your system meets the minimum requirements for a smooth experience.

Minimum Requirements

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit) (v:3.0)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5 (quad-core) or equivalent
  • RAM: 4 GB
  • GPU: Integrated graphics (optional, but recommended)
  • Storage: 200 MB of free space

Recommended Requirements

  • Processor: Intel Core i7 / AMD Ryzen 7 or higher
  • RAM: 16 GB or more
  • GPU: Dedicated GPU (NVIDIA GTX 1060 or higher)
